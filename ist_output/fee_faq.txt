Fee structure for BS programs at IST (Institute of Space Technology)

All BS programs share the same one-time charges at admission: Admission Fee Rs 35,000, Endowment Fund Rs 7,000, Library Development Fund Rs 7,000. Total one-time charges Rs 49,000 (local).

Per-semester fees vary by department/program group as follows:

1. Aerospace Engineering, Electrical Engineering, Avionics Engineering, Mechanical Engineering
   Tuition Fee Rs 151,534. Service Charges Rs 8,470. Safe/Smart Campus Rs 2,420. Total per semester Rs 162,424 (local).

2. Metallurgy and Materials Engineering
   Tuition Fee Rs 144,946. Service Charges Rs 8,470. Safe/Smart Campus Rs 2,420. Total per semester Rs 155,836 (local).

3. Computer Science, Artificial Intelligence, Data Science, Software Engineering, Computer Engineering (Computing Department)
   Tuition Fee Rs 127,962. Service Charges Rs 8,470. Safe/Smart Campus Rs 2,420. Total per semester Rs 138,852 (local).

4. Space Science, Remote Sensing and GIS
   Tuition Fee Rs 122,399. Service Charges Rs 8,470. Safe/Smart Campus Rs 2,420. Total per semester Rs 133,289 (local).

5. Biotechnology
   Tuition Fee Rs 122,399. Service Charges Rs 8,470. Safe/Smart Campus Rs 2,420. Total per semester Rs 133,289 (local).

6. Mathematics, Mathematics with Artificial Intelligence, Mathematics with Data Science
   Tuition Fee Rs 80,526. Service Charges Rs 8,470. Safe/Smart Campus Rs 2,420. Total per semester Rs 91,416 (local).

7. Physics, Chemistry (Nanotechnology)
   Tuition Fee Rs 80,526. Service Charges Rs 8,470. Safe/Smart Campus Rs 2,420. Total per semester Rs 91,416 (local).

Optional: Hostel dormitory Rs 55,000 per semester. Application processing fee (non-refundable) Rs 3,000.
Fees are subject to change; confirm latest structure on IST website.

MS and PhD programs share the same fee structure: One-time Admission Rs 25,000, Endowment Rs 7,000, Library Development Rs 7,000, Total Rs 39,000. Per semester: Tuition Rs 80,526, Service Charges Rs 4,840, Safe/Smart Campus Rs 2,420, Total per semester Rs 87,786 (local).
