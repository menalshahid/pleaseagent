Harassment Complaint Cell (HCC) - IST

IST is committed to an all-inclusive and supportive learning environment. In line with HEC Policy on Protection against Sexual Harassment in Higher Education Institutions, IST has a zero-tolerance policy for any conduct that violates the dignity of any individual.

The Harassment Complaint Cell (HCC) was established on 20 February 2012 for monitoring and protection against sexual harassment at IST. It addresses sexual and gender-based harassment. Members of the IST community feel safe and have an avenue to voice grievances.

Contact for policies or harassment issues: Dr. Rahila Naz, Head of the Harassment Complaint Cell (HCC). Room number 212, Block 2, IST. Phone 051-9075562. Email head.hcc@ist.edu.pk

Every member has the right to work and conduct learning, research, and scholarship without discrimination, harassment, or social pressure. The HCC investigative process is confidential and fair. No reprimand for filing a legitimate complaint. HCC protects the rights of complainant and accused.

Policy documents: HEC Policy Guidelines on Harassment, Protection Against Harassment of Women at the Workplace Act 2010.
